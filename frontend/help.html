Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions about ordering, payment, and delivery.

How does payment work?

DealMindanao operates on an offline payment system. When you place an order, our team reviews it and contacts you to confirm payment and delivery details. You can pay via cash on delivery (COD), bank transfer, or GCash. No online payment is required at checkout.

Why do I need to wait for confirmation?

We manually review each order to verify product availability, confirm pricing, and coordinate with our local sellers. This ensures you receive accurate information before making any payment. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.

What if my order is delayed?

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, please contact our support team at hello@dealmindanao.ph with your order number. We will track your shipment and provide an update on the status.

How do returns work?

Returns are accepted for defective, damaged, or incorrect items reported within 48 hours of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return. For full details, please review our Refund & Returns Policy.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team by:

  • Email: hello@dealmindanao.ph
  • Contact form: Contact Us page

Please include your order number for faster assistance.

Do you deliver outside Mindanao?

Currently, we serve all Mindanao regions. Delivery to other areas may be available on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team to inquire about delivery to your specific location.

Are the products authentic?

Yes. All products listed on DealMindanao are verified by our team to ensure authenticity and quality. We work directly with trusted local sellers and verify product listings before they appear on the marketplace.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's confirmed and processed. Once our team has confirmed your order and coordinated with the seller, cancellation may not be possible. Please contact support immediately if you need to cancel.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery timelines vary by location and product. Typically, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days after order confirmation and payment. Our team will provide an estimated delivery date when confirming your order.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No, there is no minimum order amount. However, some products may have minimum quantity requirements specified in their listings.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you, or explore our policies for more details.
