DealMindanao was born out of a desire to centralize the fragmented marketplace of Mindanao. We saw talented weavers in Marawi, fruit farmers in Bukidnon, and seafood producers in Zamboanga struggling to find a wider audience.

By leveraging modern technology, we've built a platform that not only sells products but tells the stories of the people behind them.
