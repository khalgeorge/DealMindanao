Refund & Returns Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Please review our return policy below.

Return Eligibility

Returns are accepted for defective, damaged, or incorrect items reported within 48 hours of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

  • Product received is defective or damaged
  • Wrong item was delivered
  • Item does not match the description

Return Process

  1. Contact our support team at hello@dealmindanao.ph within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and photos of the defective/damaged/incorrect item
  3. Our team will review your request and provide instructions
  4. Return the item in its original packaging with all accessories
  5. Once we receive and verify the returned item, we will process your refund or replacement

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items that have been used or installed
  • Items without original packaging
  • Items reported after 48 hours of delivery
  • Custom or special-order items

Refund Timeline

Refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days after we receive and verify the returned item. Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase.

For questions about returns or to initiate a return, please contact our support team.
